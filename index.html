Terms and Conditions

This is a screening assessment platform. Any form of Tele consultation, E consultation for any illness or condition is not done or  entertained here.

For and development of symptoms or and clinical condition it is best recommended to go to a Doctor physically. 

Blood and other sample testing are outsourced. Timing of the reports depends upon type of the test. However all reports will be done as the earliest possible time. 

For certain tests like 2D echo, TMT, Chest X ray, Ultrasound scan and others when necessary if asked by doctor cannot be provided at home. Necessary alternative ways to get these tests should be done by individual him/herself.

We don't ask you for any details like Adhaar, PAN, or any bank details.

Our policy is one person screening through one email. So whoever is getting screened should have a valid Email. However in case of screening of multiple members of family can be requested in advance. 

A doctor might call whenever necessary for obtaining additional history and past treatment details in few cases.
